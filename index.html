About Moatermouth

Moatermouth is the alias of Anjel Robinson, a Cincinnati-based visual development artist, illustrator, and animator. My work focuses on character-driven stories, bold color, and the line between playful and unsettling.

I explore narrative through illustration, portraiture, branding, and animation, often blending digital and traditional media. This portfolio highlights work across logo design, semi-realistic and experimental portraits, print design, and visual development for imagined worlds and characters.
